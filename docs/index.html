About Mavie

Mavie was born from a simple passion for cinema. Frustrated by the lack of a seamless platform for streaming, accessing movie info, and tracking viewing history, we decided to create our own solution.

Driven by our love for movies, Mavie is our dedication to enhancing the movie-watching experience. This project is more than just a requirement; it's a reflection of our commitment to convenience and personalized movie enjoyment.
